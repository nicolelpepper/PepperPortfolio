Get to Know Snowpack in the Yampa Valley

I directed and produced this short documentary for Yampa Valley Sustainability Council as a part of a campaign series, “Get to Know Climate in the Yampa Valley” that I developed. With the "Get to Know" series, throughout the year YVSC will explore important climate-related topics through a local lens. Using stories from our region's landscapes and community members, we hope to increase community knowledge of climate change, its impacts and solutions on a local level.
